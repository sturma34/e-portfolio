Akiva's First Year Writing E-Portfolio

I wrote a number of essays for my First Year Writing Seminar, and they were all related to running.

They included:

  1. A personal narrative describing the time I ran from Washington Heights to Battery Park.
  2. An exploratory essay that investigated the question of why runners run.
  3. A Rogerian style essay that responded to Bret Stephens' Op-Ed regarding Russian athletes being banned from the Boston Marathon.
  4. A number of other collected writings.
